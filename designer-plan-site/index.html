Honest questions

Direct answers.

Is this a protection plan?

Yes. Designer Plan is a 36-month protection program administered by Risk Assurance Partners. What’s different from the protection plans most people have run into: it isn’t sold at a retail register, the service is built around a concierge contact rather than a call-center handoff, and it’s designed for fine furniture rather than retail-volume goods.

Will my studio have to handle claims?

No. The client calls a concierge number. We field the call, the claim, and the resolution. You stay focused on the design work.

What’s actually covered?

Stains, rips, tears, burns, and accidental damage. Structural failure of frames and joints. Mechanical and electrical failure on motion components. Three tiers — Stain Protection, Premium Protection, Premium Plus Protection. Full terms are viewable before purchase on each plan card.

How does the program work?

Apply, get approved, log in. Add coverage to client projects at checkout. The system attributes the project to your studio, and your client receives the same concierge service regardless of which tier they choose. Full program terms appear in your partner account.

Can my client buy a plan directly?

Yes. Some designers add coverage at project closeout. Others send their client to the plans page to purchase directly. Either way, the service is identical — and if you’re a partner, you earn commission regardless of which path the client takes.

How much commission do designers earn?

Up to 35% per plan, depending on tier and volume. Full commission structure is detailed in your partner agreement.
