Tier one

Stain Protection

36 months of coverage for the most common household stains.

  • Common food, beverage, and accidental stains
  • Pet & human accidents
  • Ink, water marks, dirt soiling
  • Indoor furniture, bed bases, rugs
  • Coverage begins at delivery

From $[XX]/36 mo

Full coverage detail +

Designer Stain Protection offers comprehensive 36-month protection against many of the most common household stains and accidents. Save by buying direct — protecting your client’s investment should be as thoughtful as selecting it.

Coverage applies to a wide range of furniture types: indoor furniture, adjustable bed bases, and rugs. Coverage begins the moment your first eligible item is delivered, with no waiting period. This is not a basic replacement plan. Our goal is to restore damaged items to “like new” condition.

Materials covered

  • Wood & solid surface items
  • Upholstery, including leather
  • Faux leather
  • Fabric & mixed coverings

Rooms covered

  • Living room, dining room, dinette, family room
  • Bedroom, home office, accessory furniture
  • Rugs

Stains & damage covered

  • Common accidental food stains, including food grease
  • Accidental beverage, coffee, tea, wine, cocktails, liquor & water rings
  • Pet stains from non-habitual accidents (excludes odors & discoloration)
  • Human stains from non-habitual accidents (excludes odors & discoloration)
  • Accidental soiling from dirt, mud & food grease (excludes general soiling)
  • Pens (excluding markers), pencil, and dye transfer from newspaper
  • Water rings & liquid marks on wood and other hard surfaces
