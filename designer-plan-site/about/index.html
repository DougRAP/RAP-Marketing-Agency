About

A quiet layer behind your beautiful work.

Designer Plan is a 36-month protection program for the furniture interior designers specify. Coverage begins at delivery. A single concierge contact handles every claim. Designed for fine furniture, run by Risk Assurance Partners.

Who we are

Risk Assurance Partners (RAP) administers protection programs for furniture, appliances, and consumer electronics — built around a concierge-style service rather than a retail call center. Designer Plan is our program for the independent design community.

Why we built this

Designers’ names are on every install. When something goes wrong eighteen months later, the client calls the designer. We built Designer Plan to put a calm, prepared answer behind that call — one that protects the designer’s reputation, the client’s investment, and the room.
